Creative Technologist · Spatial Experience Designer

Yves Chen is a creative technologist and spatial experience designer currently pursuing a Master of Science in Architectural Technologies at SCI-Arc. Originally from Taoyuan, Taiwan, Yves blends AI, sensory interaction, and product innovation to create immersive environments beyond traditional architecture. His recent work, ÔDÔRAI, explores personalized olfactory experiences powered by smart devices and real-time biometric data.

Featured Work: ÔDÔRAI

Technical Tools

Yves integrates Rhino, Grasshopper, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Unreal Engine, coding, and 3D printing into his research and design process, exploring how emerging tools can support immersive and responsive environments.

Design Interests

Beyond architecture, Yves is deeply interested in fragrance design, experimental cooking, dessert-making, and fashion—fields that enrich his approach to multisensory experiences and personal expression.

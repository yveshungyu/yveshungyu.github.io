Product Care

To preserve your ÔDÔRAI diffuser's elegance and performance, gently wipe the exterior with a dry or lightly damp microfiber cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners. Scent modules should be stored in a cool, dry place. Regularly clean the nebulization chamber using alcohol spray to maintain optimal diffusion quality.
